Telnet/SSL Quick Connect Options

When Telnet/SSL is selected as the Protocol in the Quick Connect dialog, the connection A data path or circuit between two computers over a phone line, network cable, or other means. options are as follows:

Hostname

The hostname or IP address of the remote host.

Port

The port number of a service on a remote machine. For Telnet/SSL Telnet over SSL (Secure Sockets Layer), a cryptographic communications protocol used to encrypt network traffic over the internet. , the default port is 992.

Firewall

If your connection involves a firewall, select your firewall from the list of firewalls that have been  configured in the Global Options/Firewall dialog..
